About the Time Clock App

Our company uses a Google AppSheet-based time clock application for all employee and contractor time tracking. This mobile app allows you to clock in and out from your smartphone while ensuring you are at the correct work location.

Key Features

  • 6-State Workflow: Clock In, Break Out, Break In, Lunch Out, Lunch In, Clock Out
  • GPS Location Verification: Ensures clock activities happen at designated work sites
  • Multi-Location Support: Works across all company office and job site locations
  • Mobile-First Design: Optimized for smartphone usage
  • Real-Time Tracking: Instant updates to management dashboard

App Installation Instructions

üìß Getting Access

Management will share the app directly with your company email address. You will receive an email invitation from AppSheet with a link to access the app.

Open the Email Invitation

Check your company email for an invitation from AppSheet. Click the link in the email to open the Time Clock app in your phone's web browser.

Install as Home Screen App

For the best experience, install the app on your phone's home screen:

üì± iPhone (iOS) Instructions:

  1. Open the app link in Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right corner
  5. The app icon will now appear on your home screen

ü§ñ Android Instructions:

  1. Open the app link in Chrome browser
  2. Tap the menu button (three dots) in the top right corner
  3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Tap "Add" or "Install" to confirm
  5. The app icon will now appear on your home screen

Log In

Open the app and log in with your company Google account (the same email where you received the invitation).

Enable Location Services

When prompted, allow location access for the app. You can also manually enable this in your phone's Settings > Privacy > Location Services.

How to Use the App Daily

Arrive at Work Location

Navigate to your assigned office or job site before attempting to clock in.

Clock In

Open the app and tap "Clock In" when you arrive and are ready to start work. Always verify the action was successful before putting your phone away.

Manage Breaks & Lunch

Use "Break Out/In" and "Lunch Out/In" buttons to track all break periods. Check that each action recorded properly.

Clock Out

Tap "Clock Out" at the end of your work day while still at the work location. Confirm the clock-out was recorded before leaving.

‚ö†Ô∏è Verify Success

ALWAYS check that your clock action was successful! If any clock action fails, you must email cobey@truerootsnm.com within 24 hours to avoid losing pay.
